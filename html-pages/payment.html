Payment Methods

Cash on Delivery (COD)

We currently accept Cash on Delivery as our only payment method. Pay in cash when your order arrives at your doorstep.

How COD Works

When your order arrives, inspect your items in the presence of our delivery partner. Pay the exact order amount in cash to complete your purchase.

Order Verification

You'll receive an order confirmation email with your total amount due. Our team will call to verify your order and delivery address before dispatch.

Delivery Areas

COD is available in all major cities and surrounding areas. Contact our support team to confirm delivery availability in your location.

Contact Us

For payment-related queries, please email support@flora.com or call us at (555) 123-4567 during business hours (M-F, 9am-5pm EST).
